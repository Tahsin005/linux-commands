head - output the first part of files

    head [OPTION]... [FILE]...

tail - output the last part of files

    tail [OPTION]... [FILE]...


head cats.txt (will output first 10)
tail cats.txt (will output last 10)

head -n 1 cats.txt
tail -f 1 cats.txt

    -f, --follow[={name|descriptor}]
        output appended data as the file grows;

Another syntax:
    head -1 cats.txt
    tail -1 cats.txt

less - opposite of more

    outputs paginated data
